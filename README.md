📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis
🚀 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of marketing funnel performance using the UCI Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset. The objective was to evaluate customer conversion behavior, identify funnel drop-offs, and generate actionable insights to improve campaign effectiveness.

This project was completed as Task 3 during my Data Science & Analytics Internship at Future Interns.

🎯 Project Objectives

Analyze customer movement through the marketing funnel (Contacted → Converted)

Calculate overall conversion and drop-off rates

Identify high-performing marketing channels

Evaluate monthly campaign performance trends

Analyze customer segments based on job categories

Provide business-driven recommendations for optimization

📂 Dataset Information

Dataset Name: Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset

Source: UCI Machine Learning Repository

Data Type: Marketing campaign & customer response data

Target Variable: y (yes/no – customer conversion)

📊 Key Metrics Analyzed

✅ Overall Conversion Rate

✅ Drop-Off Rate

✅ Channel-wise Conversion Performance

✅ Monthly Conversion Trends

✅ Job Segment Conversion Analysis

🔍 Key Insights

📈 Overall Conversion Rate: ~11–12%

⚠️ Drop-Off Rate: ~88% (indicating significant funnel leakage)

📞 Telephone channel achieved the highest conversion rate (~14.6%)

📅 May and August showed the strongest campaign performance

🎯 Certain job segments (e.g., Retired, Technician) demonstrated higher conversion rates

💡 Business Recommendations

Focus marketing investment on high-performing contact channels (Telephone)

Optimize strategies for underperforming months

Target high-converting customer segments for improved ROI

Improve follow-up and engagement strategy to reduce drop-off rate
